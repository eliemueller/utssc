Destinations

Blue Mountain
Located west of Collingwood, Blue Mountain is the largest ski resort located in Ontario. From magic carpets to a terrain park that has huge sunglasses to jump over, this destination is great for all skill levels.
Sun Peaks
With an astonishing elevation of 2,080m, you will be rocking those slopes! Sun Peaks is also known as Canada's second largest ski area, with 4,270 acres spread among 3 peaks, each with a personality of its own.
Mont Tremblant
Rated the top ski resort in Québec, we hold a big organized trip to Mont Tremblant during the reading week of the season. Expect limitless activities during the trip ranging from skiing/snowboarding, skating, snowshoeing, and lots of social games!
Panorama
Being known for some of the best downhill skiing in the country, Panorama is an impressive ski resort that boasts one of North America's longest verical descents and a fantastic variety of terrain for different skill levels and abilities.
Chamonix Mont-Blanc
Nestled in the heart of the French Alps, Chamonix offers legendary off-piste skiing and breathtaking glacier views. Its rich history and diverse terrain attract skiers from around the globe seeking an extraordinary alpine adventure.

About Us

Across the three campuses of the University of Toronto, we are proud to be serving thousands of undergraduates, graduates, alumni, friends of friends, local universities and colleges, and lovers of snow. We have been working tirelessly since 2004 to provide more activities and form a stronger community network, to encourage active lifestyles and camaraderie amongst members.
